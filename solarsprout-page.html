Solar sprout

Description

Solar Sprout is a game about building, repairing, and enhancing connections in a city through applying energy in various ways. You play as Johnnie, a nonbinary robot, solving local issues.
Created for QRMJam and for the Games For Change Student Challenge.It ended up being awarded for the High School Connected Cities category as well as the Grand Prize. About 31 hours was logged into the game's development.
