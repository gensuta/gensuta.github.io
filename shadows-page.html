Shadows of huntshire

Description

Shadows of Huntshire is a choice based narrative card game. You assume the role of the high priest in the town of Huntshire, determined to sacrifice enough townspeople for the return of your lord. 

Shadows of Huntshire was developed over 14 weeks in Unity and was based on a previous prototype by Claire Wang, Luna Wu, Isabella Chen, and Zitta.
