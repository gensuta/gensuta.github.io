Rebel runner

Description

Rebel Runner is multiplayer infinite runner where you're doing rebellious things with rebellious pals! Choose one of four unique characters to destroy stuff, redistribute the wealth or run from the cops in the location of your choosing.
This game was one of the 2019 Graffiti Games for PlayNYC.
