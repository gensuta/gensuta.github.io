About me

Heya! I'm Geneva(They/Them)! I'm a game developer based in New York.

I have a lot of experience with Unity, but my love for narrative games has allowed me to get very familiar in Ren'Py and Twine.
I also have experience with 2D art and animation, creating music, and writing stories!

When I'm not creating games, I take time to roller skate, learn new recipes, and of course play a variety of games!
